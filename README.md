# FluffyFox
A 100% clientside W.I.P QOL mod for the Wynncraft Minecraft server.

## Current Features
- AntiAfk (BROKEN)
    - /ff AntiAfk or /ff aa 
    - (will) prevent being afk-kicked by automatically executing some action every couple minutes.
- Antiblind
    - /ff AntiBlind or /ff ab 
    - Cancels the effect of the blindness effect.
- AntiScroll
    - /ff NoHotbarScroll or /ff nhs
    - Blocks the scroll wheel input for scrolling through your hotbar.
- BulbHolderWaypoints
    - /ff BulbHolderWaypoints or /ff bhw
    - Creates a notification, beacon, and tracer for bulb holders in the TNA challenge.
- DiscoSwap (BROKEN)
  - /ff DiscoSwap or /ff ds
  - (will) automatically swap between your active chestplate and a Discoverer (if one is found) when a hotkey is pressed. It currently creates ghost items.
- FindItem (BROKEN)
  - /ff FindItem or /ff fi
  - (will) Automatically remember where items are stored between multiple classes, and locate them on command.
- FixSkinRendering
  - /ff FixSkinRendering or /ff fsr
  - Forces the rendering of all skin layers clientside
- FlyingAxonWarning
  - /ff FlyingAxonWarning or /ff faw 
  - Creates a notification and tracers for flying axons near you in TCC. 
- Freelook
  - /ff Freelook or /ff fl OR keybind
  - Changes your camera to a third-person perspective that does not move your players head.
- GlowingShadowlings
  - /ff GlowingShadowlings or /ff gs
  - Applies a glowing effect to shadowlings in TNA
- IcelessSnakes
  - /ff IcelessSnakes or /ff is 
  - Hides ice snakes and meteors from the mage class
- WatchedTitles
  - /ff WatchedTitles or /ff wt
  - Creates colored titles as an alternative to the audio or particle telegraphs for the TNA boss watched phase.
